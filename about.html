THE MOVEMENT

BEYOND CLOTHING.
A CULTURAL IDENTITY.

Zaylore Studio is not just a clothing brand; it is a premium fashion movement and a cultural streetwear identity. We are building a future global luxury streetwear label designed for those who refuse to blend in.

Our vision is rooted in originality, youth culture influence, and modern minimal aesthetic language. From our signature oversized t-shirts and hoodies to meticulously crafted cargo pants, joggers, and bomber jackets, every piece is a testament to premium oversized fashion and modern street culture.

FOUNDER & BRAND AUTHORITY

PM MOHAMMED WAAIZ

Founder, Owner & Managing Director

At the core of Zaylore Studio is the vision of PM Mohammed Waaiz — a modern founder and creative entrepreneur focused on building a globally influential premium streetwear identity rooted in originality, ambition, and cultural expression.

His vision extends beyond fashion products. Zaylore Studio represents individuality, modern youth culture, elevated street aesthetics, oversized fashion, and the emotional connection between identity and self-expression.

Inspired by global urban streetwear culture, modern design philosophy, contemporary fashion, music, art, and digital creativity, PM Mohammed Waaiz founded Zaylore Studio with the ambition of creating a luxury streetwear India label that feels authentic, visionary, and culturally connected.

THE BRAND REFLECTS:

  • Confidence without arrogance
  • Creativity without limitation
  • Individuality without compromise
  • Luxury with cultural relevance

As Founder, Owner & Managing Director, PM Mohammed Waaiz is building Zaylore Studio not as a trend-driven fashion label, but as a long-term creative movement designed to influence modern streetwear culture globally.

LEADERSHIP PHILOSOPHY:

  • Originality over imitation
  • Vision over validation
  • Quality over mass production
  • Culture over noise
  • Long-term legacy over temporary hype

Through premium oversized silhouettes, modern streetwear storytelling, and elevated fashion direction, Zaylore Studio aims to become a globally recognized premium streetwear house representing ambition, creativity, youth culture, and authentic self-expression.
